Referral FAQ

How do I get a referral code?

Go to the Get a Code page, enter your name, and a code is generated for you instantly — format FHI-NAME-001. No signup or approval required.

How does my friend use my code?

They enter your code at checkout on any Francisco Holdings product — PrimeDox AI, OmniaGuard, CCLDR, TechPetCage, or Vault Velocity Auto — and get 10% off. If a product's checkout doesn't have a code field, they can include it in the PayPal payment note instead (e.g. "Referral: FHI-DEREK-001").

How much do I earn?

You earn a 15% commission on every sale made with your code, once Derek confirms the payment cleared.

When and how do I get paid?

Commissions are paid out via PayPal, typically within 48 hours of a confirmed sale. This is an honor-system process reviewed manually by Derek — there is no automated payout.

How is this tracked?

Sales made through a checkout that logs your code are recorded automatically. You can check what's been logged for your code on the Check My Earnings page. Purchases made via a direct PayPal link without a code entered will not appear there, even if Derek pays you for them manually after the fact.

Is this a guaranteed payout system?

No. This is a best-effort log, not a binding ledger. Always confirm your actual earnings and payout directly with Derek. The tracking page exists to give you visibility, not to replace that conversation.

Can discounts stack?

Yes — the referred customer's 10% off is stackable with other applicable discounts (senior, veteran, fixed income, etc.) where those apply on a given product.
