The Problem

Three trillion-dollar markets. Zero unified provider.

Constitutional and cannabis-law education, cybersecurity, and AI-driven business automation are each served by fragmented, disconnected vendors — forcing clients to stitch together legal counsel, security tooling, and automation software from unrelated providers with no shared trust layer.

Legal

Fragmented Counsel

Cannabis-law and constitutional-rights education is scattered across independent practitioners with no consistent, AI-augmented delivery system.

Security

Disjointed Defense

Small and mid-size operators patch together point-solution security tools instead of one coherent, AI-monitored guard layer.

Automation

Siloed Tooling

Business automation platforms rarely speak to legal or security systems — every integration is a custom, costly rebuild.

The Solution

One AI-powered holding company. Every division connected.

Francisco Holdings Inc. is a vertically integrated holding structure spanning legal education, cybersecurity, and automation — each division built AI-first and cross-linked through shared infrastructure, shared brand trust, and a shared technology stack.

Vertical Integration

Legal, security, and automation divisions share one technology backbone instead of operating as disconnected acquisitions.

AI-First by Design

Every flagship product is built around an AI concierge or AI monitoring layer from day one — not retrofitted onto legacy software.

One Brand Umbrella

45 companies and divisions operate under a single holding structure, compounding trust and cross-sell opportunity across the portfolio.
